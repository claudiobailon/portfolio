About Me

I am a new software developer currently studying at Code Fellows. I've worked a variety of different jobs, and my experiences have helped me hone my ability to quickly learn to do a new job or pick up a new skill. Most recently, I worked as a surveyor, and started with no experience in a field that usually requires a 2 year technical degree. I was able to learn on the job very quickly and even help my coworkers learn new software. Seeing how two different programs approach the same tasks will be helpful to me as a developer. With work slowing because of COVID-19, I decided to jump into software development, which I tried briefly in college and always wanted to come back to. Software development is appealing to me because it is so diverse and has a lot of opportunity, which means I can constantly learn and try new things. I'm looking to find somewhere that I can contribute to a good team while also learning and continue to grow as a developer and a person.
